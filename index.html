About CompassView Guesthouse

Welcome to CompassView Guesthouse, your comfortable home away from home in the charming Karoo town of Nieu-Bethesda, South Africa. Our guesthouse offers quality accommodation with stunning views and warm hospitality, making it the perfect base to explore this historic town and its famous Owl House.

Our Location

Discover Nieu-Bethesda

CompassView Guesthouse is perfectly located in the heart of Nieu-Bethesda, a picturesque Karoo town famous for its artistic heritage and the renowned Owl House.

  • Walking distance to the famous Owl House
  • Close to local restaurants and galleries
  • Perfect for stargazing in the clear Karoo skies
  • Ideal base for exploring the Great Karoo
